Dear Parents and Friends of TCMS:
  
On behalf of the Students, Staff and Governing Board of Terrace Community Middle School, please accept our heartfelt thank you for your support during our annual Team Tornado fundraising campaign.  You continue to amaze us with your generosity and support of our school.  We have raised a total of $99,810. Please check our website to see all the wonderful families and businesses who made a significant contribution to our school. The money raised from Team Tornado will support our efforts in building an additional pavilion for our students as well as other projects to support student learning throughout the school year. We are currently receiving bids for the construction for our pavilion and can’t wait to get started.

Now, we are celebrating the Team Tornado success with our students. In addition to the weekly rewards and incentives that students enjoyed during the month of September and October, the homerooms that achieved 100% participation and the $3500.00 class goal, as well as students who donated $1000 or more, will be going to Camp Cristina on October 27th or November 3rd to participate in some great activities including “Quidditch”.

Lastly, we are most excited about our 3rd annual TCMS lock-in.  All students who contributed donations totaling $300 or more will be invited to attend the lock-in on November 9th from 8:00PM to 7:00AM on November 10th. This will be another amazing night with lots of activities, food and fun to culminate the conclusion of another great Team Tornado. Thank you again for your continued support.

 

Please visit the Business Directory of Top Level Donors.


Best Regards,
 
Tahvia Shaw, M.Ed, Principal
