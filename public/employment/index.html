Employment

BOARD POSTING

The Back to Basics Charter School Foundation Board of Directors announces it will accept applications for a vacancy on the Board. Applicants should email their resume and a Letter of Explanation of their interest in this position to Lew Kirk at lnebkirk@gmail.com AND Tahvia Shaw at tahvia.shaw@tcmstornadoes. Special consideration will be given to candidates with grant writing experience and/or business/corporate leadership experience. Deadline for submissions are March 3rd, 2017 at 2PM.

Online Application & Resume Upload Link

Highly energetic and professional, our faculty members teach a total of 5 classes per day (22 per classroom) and are asked to be available before and after school for extra help tutoring. The salary is competitive with that of the school district, and we offer a great benefits package. Applicants should have a minimum of 2 years of experience (not required), hold a professional or temporary teaching certificate issued by the State of Florida (or a Statement of Eligibility), and be highly qualified.
